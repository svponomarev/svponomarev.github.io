Project for the personal training studio
Volna Sport Club

  • Development of an application for recording and analyzing the readings of the electromyographic sensors during a series of exercises;
  • Development of an application for the integration of three types of reports (electromyographic report, mobility report, tensometry report) and analysis of the plasticity of the athlete's anatomy trains;
  • Development of a mobile application with three-dimensional visualization of problematic anatomy trains on the human body and generation of workouts with recommended exercises
